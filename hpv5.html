About Us

At Heaven Picked Vintage, we love vintage. Even better, we LOVE affordable vintage. Now, there’s many places to go for that, but the BEST option? Heaven…. Picked……. Vintage.

We are passionate about bringing you unique and stylish pieces from bygone eras. Each item in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, character, and timeless appeal. Located in the beautiful city of Chilliwack, British Columbia, we aim to make sustainable fashion accessible and exciting for everyone.

Thank you for choosing Heaven Picked Vintage. We hope you find something you truly love!
